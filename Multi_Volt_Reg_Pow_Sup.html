Multi Voltage Regulated Power Supply

Overview

Design a multi voltage regulated power supply circuit with two dual fixed DC output (+ 5V and + 12V), and perform simulation and testing on the circuit designed.

Tools

NI Multisim

Methodology/Concept

Linear Regulation

To have DC output, linear regulation is the desired concept. This required the use of linear regulator such as LM7805. Linear regulators are used to maintain a constant output voltage even when there are fluctuations in the input voltage or changes in the load by adjusting the resistance in the circuit to compensate for any variations, ensuring that the output voltage remains stable.
