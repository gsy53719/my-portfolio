4 to 16 Balanced Tree Decoder

Overview

Analyse combinational and sequential logic circuits and registers in integrated circuit technology and design logic circuits and registers based on digital circuit modelling. Develop two circuits, in which one of them acts as the original circuit, while another one should be the improved version of the original circuit that is more efficient.

Tools

Xininx Vivado

Methodology/Concept

Adding Hierarchy

Adding hierarchy can improve the design significantly in terms of both speed and area. In this case, adding hierarchy technique was applied to the balanced tree decoder by collapsing the hierarchy of the decoder to achieve more efficient synthesis.

Results

Functional Block Diagram/Elaborated View.

The structure of the improved design has more hierarchy than the original design.

Output Waveform.

The output waveform for both design are the same when the same input is given.

Resource Utilization.

Improved design has lower number of Look Up Table (LUT) and achieve lower percentage of utilization.
